Sheltered Homelessness

Providing safety and protection

Sheltered Homelessness is a non-profit organisation dedicated to helping local communities look more clean and presenatble by ensuring that homeless people are provided shelter and not parading the streets.

About Sheltered Homelessness:

This organization has been founded by Rifumo Mabasa to help homeless people keep warm during this winter season by providing them with shelter and access to basic foods to help to maintain their health and ensure that there are fewer homeless people patrolling the streets to maintain a clean and healthy environment. Through this organization we plan to ensure that there is less homelessness in the streets and to ensure that homeless people are provided with houses and shelter. This Organization is centered towards homeless people and the ensuring of their safety -

We intend to ensure that communities are presenatble and are ingood condition and one of the ways we plan on implementing that is by making sure that there is less Homeless people parding the streets. This helps bith parties as now local members that live in the community will be living in a safer and cleaner area, homeless people will now be provided with protection and shelter.

Goals and Objective

With this Organization we determined to achieve a goal of developing a website that allows locals of the area to reach out and report any homeless people patrolling the area allowing us to help them get the proper care and shelter they need to keep warm, safe and allowing them access to foods and water in order to maintain their health. We plan on making this website attract more attention to the public by advertising this Organization to the people worldwide by posting billboards, TV commercials and making social media accounts to make it by the public eye. These advertisements will provide the website and information about the Organization and how it benefits the people. Our goal is to Increase awareness about and educate the public about homelessness and highlight the organization's impact in reducing homelessness. The corresponding objective which is to create a website equipped with blogs and newsletter subscription for raising awareness about and educate the public about homelessness and highlight the organization's impact in reducing homelessness.
